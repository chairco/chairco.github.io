About Me

Yi-Chieh Chen(Jason) earned his master degree at the graduate school of Institute of Healthcare Information Management in CCU. He has served a senior software R & D engineer for 5 years at Pegatron. He is also the Program Chair of PyCon Taiwan 2017.

He love Python’s clean, and had a sense of achievement when he finished the complex work in the clean code. Yi-Chieh like using Django framework to make system for other engineers to get done job more easily and also like to integration kind of different tools together. Recent now he also interested in research Machine Learning and Deep Learning.

Yi-Chieh Chen’s blog is https://chairco.com.tw, although it’s not updated frequently, he will like to share on here if he has anything new find out

專長

我的專長是軟體開發與統計分析。參與過為開發電子產品的測試軟體的專案，內容包含自動化測試、韌體(Firmware)或是驅動程式(driver)開發，與測試流程整合。最近兩年則擔任網頁全端工程師，負責企業內部 IT 資訊系統開發。任務是設計 Web 流程與 BackEnd 的演算法。

我的碩士畢業論文醫院評鑑知識管理系統成功模式之驗證是透過建置 Model 理論來驗證醫院的知識管理系統，使用了很多統計分析技術如假設檢定、迴歸分析、結構方程模式等。因此統計屬於我的第二專長。
